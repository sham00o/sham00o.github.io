Tracks

Importing an EPUB

You can provide an EPUB to unlock offline capabilities, as well as provide footnotes and outlines to the app

Tracks only knows how to read the Holy Bible Recovery Version. So before you start, you'll need to have purchased this specific Google Play eBook. If you purchased this on Apple Books, you are welcome to try but your results may vary.

Download EPUB as ACSM

After you've purchased the ebook, you can download it to a desktop from Google Play Books or Apple Books.

What you have just downloaded is actually an ACSM file. So you'll need to go to the next step.

Open ACSM for the real EPUB

An ACSM is essentially a receipt, or voucher, from Adobe for your eBook. You can use Adobe Digital Editions to show you own the eBook and exchange the voucher for the ePub file.

Remove DRM encryption

Tracks won't be able to read the file if you skipped this step, because the file is encrypted. The DRM only allows you to read your eBook in specific programs.

Removing this will allow you to share the file across your personal devices and access the file in any program or application. Once you've done this, the file is ready to be imported into Tracks!

For step-by-step instructions you can read follow this link or look it up on Google
